# Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting
## ABTalks 60-Day Claude AI Mastery Challenge

## What I Learned Today
Role-Based Prompting means telling Claude to act as a specific
expert before asking your question. It changes the tone, depth,
and quality of the answer completely.

## Without Role Prompt
Prompt: "How do I grow my business?"
Output: Generic tips — marketing, networking, product quality...

## With Role Prompt
Prompt: "You are a startup founder with 10 years experience.
How do I grow my business?"
Output: Focus on product-market fit. Build in public.
Your first 100 customers are your co-founders.

## Personas I Explored
- Developer — code logic and systems thinking
- Product Manager — user stories and roadmaps
- HR Manager — people-first policy advice
- Founder — strategic vision and growth
- Marketer — audience and brand language

## 3 Key Benefits
1. Get expert answers without hiring an expert
2. Control the tone — technical, simple, or strategic
3. Switch roles instantly for multiple perspectives

## Key Takeaway
"Don't just ask Claude — assign Claude."
