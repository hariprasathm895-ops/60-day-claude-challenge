# Day 8 – Personal Environmental Health Analyzer
## ABTalks 60-Day Claude AI Mastery Challenge

## What I Learned Today
Used Claude as a Senior Data Analyst + Dashboard Developer
to build a fully interactive Environmental Health Analyzer
with real Indian city AQI and water quality data.

## What I Built
A complete HTML dashboard with:
- AQI data for 10 Indian cities
- Interactive city selector and filters
- 4 live charts (AQI, PM2.5, PM10, City Ranking)
- Personal Health Report Card (Air, Water, Skin, Hair)
- Personalized recommendations per city
- Dark theme professional UI

## Cities Analyzed
Delhi (287), Kolkata (212), Ahmedabad (198),
Chennai (158), Mumbai (168), Hyderabad (112),
Bangalore (95), Pune (88), Coimbatore (72), Mysuru (48)

## Key Findings
- Cleanest: Mysuru (48 AQI) — beats WHO standards
- Most Polluted: Delhi (287 AQI) — 3.6x national average
- My City Coimbatore: 72 AQI — Satisfactory

## Key Learnings
1. Claude can build full interactive dashboards in one prompt
2. Role-based prompting gives professional quality output
3. Data analysis + UI design combined = powerful results
4. Claude Artifacts can generate downloadable HTML apps
